Lasagna

Mary's delicious Lasagna recipe.

Mary's delicious lasagna is a comforting, mouthwatering dish layered with love and rich flavors. Each bite offers a perfect balance of tender pasta sheets, savory ground meat, tangy marinara sauce, and a generous blend of melted mozzarella, ricotta, and Parmesan cheese. The top is golden and slightly crispy, while the inside remains warm, creamy, and packed with seasoned goodness. Baked to perfection, Mary’s lasagna fills the kitchen with an irresistible aroma that promises a hearty, satisfying meal every time. It's the kind of dish that brings people together and leaves everyone asking for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (or Italian sausage)
  • 1/2 chopped stinky onion
  • 6 cloves stinky garlic
  • can of carinara
  • Gwooten free noodles
  • all the cheese you want
  • lots of love

Steps to make the lasagna

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C)
  2. Cook the noodles (if not using oven-ready):
  3. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  4. Boil lasagna noodles until al dente (about 8–10 minutes), then drain and set aside.
  5. Prepare the meat sauce:
  6. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  7. Add chopped onion and cook until soft.
  8. Add garlic and sauté for 1 minute.
  9. Add ground beef (or sausage), breaking it up as it cooks. Cook until browned.
  10. Drain excess grease if needed.
  11. Stir in marinara sauce and simmer for 5–10 minutes. Season with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
  12. Mix the cheese filling:
  13. In a medium bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, 1/4 cup of Parmesan, and a pinch of salt. Mix until smooth.
  14. Assemble the lasagna:
  15. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  16. Place 3 noodles over the sauce.
  17. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  18. Sprinkle with mozzarella.
  19. Repeat layers (sauce → noodles → ricotta → mozzarella) two more times.
  20. Finish with remaining meat sauce and a generous layer of mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  21. Bake:
  22. Cover the dish with foil (lightly tented so it doesn’t touch the cheese).
  23. Bake for 25 minutes, then remove foil.
  24. Bake uncovered for an additional 15–20 minutes, or until the top is bubbly and golden.
  25. Rest:
  26. Let the lasagna cool for 10–15 minutes before slicing. This helps it set up and makes serving easier.
  27. Serve and enjoy:
  28. Garnish with fresh parsley or basil if desired.
  29. Serve with garlic bread or a green salad for a full meal!
